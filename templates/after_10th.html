Tips for Choosing Right Stream

Follow Your Interest: Choose a stream that interests you. You'll study these subjects for 2 years, so passion matters!
Know Your Strengths: Consider your strongest subjects. If you're good at math, Science or Commerce with Math is good. If you enjoy creative writing, Arts might be perfect.
Research Careers: Explore what careers each stream leads to. Talk to professionals or counselors about job opportunities.
Consider Your Family: Discuss with family about financial aspects and their expectations, but make the final decision yourself.
Keep Backup Options: You can change paths later, but it's easier to plan ahead. Many careers accept students from different streams.
