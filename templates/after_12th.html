Important Tips for 12th Pass Students

Start Early: Begin researching careers and colleges as soon as possible. Entrance exams require months of preparation.
Entrance Exams: Most professional courses require entrance exams. Prepare systematically - JEE, NEET, CAT, CLAT, etc.
College Research: Don't just choose a course - research colleges too. Placement records, faculty, and infrastructure matter.
Internships: Start gaining experience through internships during your studies. It helps in final placements.
Government vs Private: Consider both government and private sector jobs. Government jobs offer stability, private offers growth.
Online Courses: Supplement your degree with online certifications from Coursera, Udemy, NPTEL, etc.
