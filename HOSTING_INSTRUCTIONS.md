# 🚀 FREE HOSTING GUIDE - Career Guidance AI App

## Option 1: PythonAnywhere (RECOMMENDED - FREE)

### Steps:
1. Go to **www.pythonanywhere.com**
2. Click **"Register"** → Create free account
3. After login, go to **"Web"** tab
4. Click **"Add a new web app"**
5. Select **"Flask"** and Python version **"Python 3.10"**
6. Click through (don't configure yet)
7. After created, click on your app → **"Go to directory"**
8. Upload these files:
   - app.py
   - knowledge_base.py
   - translations.py
   - database.db
   - requirements.txt
   - Create folders: static/, templates/
   - Upload contents to respective folders
9. Go to **"Web"** tab again
10. Click **"Reload"** your app
11. Click your URL (like yourusername.pythonanywhere.com)!

**Your app will be live for FREE!**

---

## Option 2: Render (FREE)

1. Go to **render.com**
2. Sign up with GitHub
3. Create "Web Service"
4. Connect your GitHub repo or upload files
5. Build command: `pip install -r requirements.txt`
6. Start command: `python app.py`
7. Get free URL!

---

## Option 3: Glitch (FREE & EASY)

1. Go to **glitch.com**
2. Sign in and click "New Project"
3. Choose "Import from GitHub" or upload files
4. Your app will be live instantly!
5. Get your URL from the project
